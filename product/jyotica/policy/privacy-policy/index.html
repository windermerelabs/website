2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of data:

a) Information you provide

  • Account details from sign-in (e.g. name, email, profile photo) via Google Sign-In.
  • Birth details you enter — date of birth, time of birth, and place of birth — used to generate charts, predictions, and recommendations.
  • Chat messages, questions, prompts, and other content you submit to the AI features.
  • Any feedback, support requests, or reports you send us.
  • Location (only with your permission) — the place/location you enter and, only if you explicitly grant location access, your device location — used for panchang, muhurta, and sunrise/sunset calculations. We do not collect device location automatically; you control this permission and can turn it off at any time in your device settings.

b) Information collected automatically

  • IP address and device information — device model, operating system and version, app version, language, time zone, and similar technical identifiers.
  • Analytics data — feature usage, screens viewed, taps, session activity, streaks, and engagement events.
  • Crash and diagnostic logs — error reports, stack traces, and performance data used to detect and fix problems.
  • Push notification tokens — to deliver notifications you have enabled.

We do not intentionally collect special-category data beyond the birth details and location you choose to provide. Please do not submit confidential, sensitive, or unnecessary personal information in chats.

3. Why We Collect It (Purposes)

We use your information to:

  • Provide, personalize, and operate the App's features and your charts/readings.
  • Generate AI responses to your queries.
  • Authenticate you and secure your account.
  • Send notifications you have enabled.
  • Measure and improve the Service (analytics).
  • Debug, monitor, and fix errors and abuse.
  • Maintain security, prevent fraud and misuse, and comply with legal obligations.

We may also use your data to improve our services, for debugging, and for analytics. We do not use your personal data or chats to train AI models.

4. Human Review

  • debug, maintain, and improve the Service;

  • comply with legal obligations.

Access is limited to authorized personnel and service providers on a need-to-know basis.

6. Chat Storage

To be explicit about how your chats are handled:

  • Chats may be stored on your device and/or on our servers.
  • Chats may be processed automatically (including by AI providers) to generate responses.
  • Chats may be reviewed by humans for debugging, security, and abuse prevention.
  • Chats may be retained for security, audit, and legal-compliance purposes.
  • Chats may be used to debug and improve the Service. We do not use your chats to train AI models.

Please do not include confidential, financial, medical, or other sensitive personal information in your chats. We display a warning in the chat for this reason. If you voluntarily share personal or sensitive information in your messages, you do so at your own risk and are responsible for it.

9. Your Rights & Account Deletion

Subject to applicable law, you may request to access, correct, or delete your personal data, and to object to or restrict certain processing.

Account deletion. You can request deletion of your account from within the App or by emailing us (Section 11). When you delete your account:

  • Deletion is permanent and immediate. Your account is closed straight away, and you will not be able to sign in or register again with it. There is no grace period, and deletion cannot be undone — please save anything you want to keep before deleting.
  • We delete or anonymize your personal data — such as your profile, birth details, and chats — within 90 days (and longer where necessary for security, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, backups, audit, or to comply with legal obligations).
  • We permanently retain a minimal account record — including your account identifier (such as your user ID and the email address linked to the account) and the deletion date. We keep this indefinitely to enforce the permanent closure of your account, prevent re-registration, recognize returning users, and protect against fraud and abuse.
  • Some data already shared with or processed by third-party providers (e.g. AI providers such as OpenAI), to limit this, we do not share identifying details such as your exact name or contact information with AI providers — only the birth details needed to generate responses, which on their own do not identify you personally.

To exercise any right, contact us at the address in Section 11. We may need to verify your identity before acting on a request.
